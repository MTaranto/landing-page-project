Meet new Galaxy M62

Looks as good as it feels

The sleek design of Galaxy M62 gives it an eye-catching, prismatic look. Refined curves make it comfortable to hold and provides easy screen navigation. A holographic pattern and premium gloss finish create a dynamic look from any angle and comes in Black, Green or Blue.

A richer viewing experience through spectacularly vivid color

Immerse yourself in the 6.7-inch Infinity-O Display of Galaxy M62, where its near bezel-less design creates a wider aspect ratio for a bigger, better view. Experience everything you love in vibrant high-definition and true-to-life color with FHD+ Super AMOLED Plus display technology.

A quad camera for better image quality and less missed moments

Galaxy M62 multi-lens camera system takes photos to the next level. Go ultra high-res with a 64MP Main Camera for crisp, clear photos throughout the day. Expand the viewing angle with Ultra Wide Camera. Customize focus with Depth Camera, or get closer to the details with Macro Camera.

Macro Camera pays attention to the tiny details

Get in close with the 5MP Macro Camera and discover details you may have missed otherwise. It applies natural bokeh effect to your pictures, allowing your subject to stand out from the background.

Power-up your performance with a super fast processor

Blast through one task or tackle multitasking with ease. The advanced Octa-core processor and up to 8GB of RAM deliver powerful graphics performance for immersive gaming. Plus, keep your files and photos on hand with 128GB internal storage — then add up to 1TB more via microSD card.

Meet the 7,000mAh¹ monster battery

With a battery this big, you've got monster power in your hand. The 7,000mAh¹ capacity lets you spend your time doing what you love and worry less about battery life. And when it does finally tell you to plug in, 25W Super Fast Charging brings it back to full power in a hurry.

Don’t settle for less when it comes to security

Built into the phone's hardware and software from the start, Samsung Knox protects your phone from the minute it's turned on. Offering multi-layered security, it defends your most sensitive information from malware and malicious threats.
